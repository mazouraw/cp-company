50 fili gommato goggle jacket

This 50 Fili gommato “hinged watchviewer” Goggle Jacket from 1996 was a perfect replica of Osti’s 1988 original with the exception of the watchviewer detail. A key piece from the “archive” section from that year's collection, it was described as being dedicated to 'the nostalgists' by a staff member of C.P. Company at the time.
