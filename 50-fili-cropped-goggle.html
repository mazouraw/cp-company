50 fili cropped goggle jacket

One of the most interesting shapes from the only winter collection Osti dedicated to the Google Jacket. This cropped parka is lined with a “Fix” treated wool, has collar and hood detailing reminiscent of the origi-nal Inuit parka designs first adopted by Norwegian armed forces and the possibility to fold away and out of sight the famous goggles. From the collection of the Westminster Menswear Archive.
