beekeper jacket

Without Urban Protection branding but from the same period and spirit. Modelled on an apiarist’s coat this so called “Bee-keeper” model expresses the hybrid poetic spirit with which Ferrari designed his collection. This model in particular, he says, was inspired by the tension between rural and urban and was dedicated to Italian poet, author, intellectual and filmmaker Pier Paolo Pasolini.
