garment dyed nylon short jacket

The finest remaining example of C.P. Company’s first experiments in garment dyeing nylon. Using a nylon-only garment dyeing recipe, the cotton takes on a particular interesting “faded” tone of the nylon’s green, highlighted through the design of an integrated button-up vest.
